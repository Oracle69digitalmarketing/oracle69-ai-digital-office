# 1. Executive Summary

Oracle69 AI Digital Office is an AI-native enterprise operating system where intelligent AI employees collaborate as a real digital organization.

Unlike traditional AI assistants that answer isolated prompts, Oracle69 AI Digital Office manages complete business workflows through specialized AI departments coordinated by a central Execution Engine.

The objective is to help organizations reduce operational overhead, improve execution quality, preserve institutional knowledge, and increase productivity through intelligent collaboration.

---

# 2. Product Vision

Build an AI workforce capable of operating as an organization's digital office.

The platform should coordinate work, manage knowledge, execute projects, support employees, and continuously improve organizational performance.
