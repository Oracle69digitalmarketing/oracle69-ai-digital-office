# 1. Executive Summary

The AI Agent Architecture defines how intelligent digital employees operate, collaborate, make decisions, share knowledge, and execute work inside Oracle69 AI Digital Office.

Unlike traditional AI assistants that function independently, Oracle69 AI Digital Office is designed as a structured organization where AI employees operate under defined authority, responsibilities, workflows, and governance.

Every AI employee is treated as an operational department rather than a standalone chatbot.

---

# 2. Architecture Vision

The objective is to build an AI workforce that behaves like a real organization.

Every AI employee should:

Understand its role.

Know its responsibilities.

Know its authority.

Know when to escalate.

Collaborate with other departments.

Learn from completed work.

Operate according to organizational policies.
