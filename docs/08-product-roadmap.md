# 1. Executive Summary

The Product Roadmap defines the phased evolution of Oracle69 AI Digital Office from an initial Minimum Viable Product (MVP) into a fully autonomous AI-native enterprise operating system.

The roadmap prioritizes delivering measurable business value early while establishing a scalable foundation for future capabilities.

Each phase introduces additional AI employees, workflow intelligence, integrations, and enterprise features without disrupting the core architecture.

---

# 2. Product Vision

Build the world's most capable AI workforce platform where organizations operate through intelligent digital employees that collaborate, learn, and continuously improve.

---

# 3. Strategic Objectives

The roadmap is designed to:

• Deliver a usable MVP quickly

• Validate market demand

• Improve organizational execution

• Expand AI workforce capabilities

• Enable enterprise adoption

• Build an ecosystem platform
