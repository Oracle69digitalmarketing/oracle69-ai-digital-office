# 1. Executive Summary

Oracle69 AI Digital Office is an AI-native enterprise operating system that enables organizations to operate with intelligent digital employees instead of disconnected software tools.

The business model combines Software-as-a-Service (SaaS), AI usage, professional services, enterprise licensing, and industry-specific solutions to create multiple recurring revenue streams.

The long-term objective is to become the operating system for AI-powered organizations.

---

# 2. Vision

Create the world's leading platform for AI-powered organizations where every business can deploy a complete digital workforce.

---

# 3. Mission

Help organizations eliminate operational inefficiencies through intelligent AI employees that execute business processes reliably, securely, and collaboratively.
